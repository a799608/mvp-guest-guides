proportions • percents • unit rates

💎 Tonight: Test Prep Mode

📚 Cheat Sheet — read this first

💡 Proportion = two fractions equal to each other (like 2/3 = 4/6)

✏️ Cross multiply to solve: if a/b = c/d, then a×d = b×c

💯 Percent setup: part/whole = percent/100 (works for ALL percent problems)

⚡ Unit rate = how much for ONE thing (price per ounce, miles per hour)

🛍️ Better buy = whichever has the lower unit rate

📐 Similar shapes have proportional sides — set up a proportion to find missing sides

🎵 Section 1: For You Page Math

Quick refresher: cross-multiply to solve. ab = cd means a×d = b×c.

You played "Cruel Summer" 3 times in 12 minutes on the way to school. At that pace, how many times will it loop on a 35-minute drive to your friend’s house?
Set up: 312 = x35

💡 Cross multiply: 12 · x = 3 · 35

A TikTok creator gets 6 likes for every 25 views. If a video has 500 views, how many likes did it get?
625 = x500

💡 Cross multiply: 25x = 6 · 500

Your friendship bracelet uses 7 pink beads for every 4 white beads. You’re using 28 pink beads. How many white beads do you need?

💡 Set up: pink/white = 7/4 = 28/x

🛍️ Section 3: Sephora-Level Percents

Use the magic setup for ALL percent problems:

partwhole = %100

"of" = whole, "is" = part. Plug in what you know, solve for what you don’t.

25% of what number is 28?

💡 x is the WHOLE here. 25/100 = 28/x

What percent of 72 is 18?
Type just the number.

💡 % is unknown: x/100 = 18/72

60% of what number is 45?

💡 60/100 = 45/x. Cross multiply

What is 60% of 12?

💡 x is the part: x/12 = 60/100

Drunk Elephant Lala Retro is $74 normally. Sephora is running it 25% off. How much do you SAVE?
Round to nearest cent.

💡 Find the savings: 25/100 = x/74

You crushed it on a quiz: 23 right out of 25. What percent did you get?
Type just the number.

💡 x/100 = 23/25

Mallory is knitting a blanket. She wants it 10 ft long, and it’s currently 5.75 ft. What percent is done?

💡 part/whole = 5.75/10

Anthony makes $3,200/month and pays 28% of his income on rent. How much is rent?

💡 x is the rent (part). 28/100 = x/3200

🎤 Section 4: Concert/Word Problem Mode

Set up a KEY first (what’s on top, what’s on bottom), then write the proportion. Then cross multiply.

The park ranger keeps the fishing pond at a ratio of 4 sunfish to 3 perch. He puts in 300 sunfish. How many perch does he need?

💡 sunfish/perch = 4/3 = 300/x

Sandra reads 5 pages every 4 minutes. How many pages can she read in 1 hour?
(1 hour = 60 minutes)

💡 pages/minutes = 5/4 = x/60

Two similar triangles. Small triangle sides: x and 12. Big triangle matching sides: 56 and 84. Find x.

💡 small/small = big/big → x/12 = 56/84

Green paint is 5 parts yellow to 3 parts blue. You’re using 2 cans of yellow. How many cans of blue?

💡 yellow/blue = 5/3 = 2/x

Your morning skincare routine takes 9 minutes for 6 steps. If your nighttime routine has 10 steps, how long should it take? (assume same pace)

💡 minutes/steps: 9/6 = x/10

You earn 8 followers for every 30 minutes of livestreaming. After 2 hours (120 min), how many followers did you gain?

💡 followers/minutes = 8/30 = x/120

⚡ Section 5: Unit Rate Speed Round

Unit rate = how much for ONE. Just divide the total by the number of units.

A tire shop charged a truck driver $5,400 to replace 18 tires. What’s the cost per tire?

💡 total ÷ count: 5400 / 18

It took Hank 5 minutes to fill his 40-gallon gas tank. How many gallons per minute?

💡 40 / 5

A flight from Philly to Vegas (2,177 miles) takes 5 hours 45 minutes. What’s the unit rate (mph)? Round to nearest tenth.
(5h 45m = 5.75 hours)

💡 miles / hours = 2177 / 5.75

Spotify Wrapped: you played 8,400 minutes across 350 songs this year. What’s the average minutes per song?

💡 total minutes ÷ song count

Too Faced mascara: $29 for a tube that lasts 3 months. Cost per month? Round to nearest cent.

💡 29 / 3

💸 Section 6: Better Buy Battles

Find the unit rate for each option. Lower price per unit = better buy.

Carla can buy a 32-lb bag of dog food for $62.95 OR a 70-lb bag for $126.95. Which is the better buy? (Type 32 or 70)

💡 cost ÷ pounds for each, then compare

Fenty Gloss Bomb: $20 for 0.3 oz. Glossier Lip Gloss: $14 for 0.14 oz. Which is the better deal per ounce? (Type fenty or glossier)

💡 price ÷ ounces for each. Lower = better.

Movie theater popcorn: small is $6 for 32 oz. Large is $9 for 85 oz. Which is the better buy? (Type small or large)

💡 cost ÷ ounces for each
