🤔 Section 8: Which Measure of Central Tendency?

Mode = best for CATEGORIES (favorites, most popular)
Mean = best for balanced numbers (no outliers)
Median = best when there IS an outlier (one extreme number)

Example: "Most popular shoe brand?" → You want the most common answer → Mode

🌡 Section 9: Temperature & Average Rate

Temperature: °C = (°F - 32) × 5/9
Step 1: Subtract 32. Step 2: Multiply by 5/9.

Average Rate: Rate = Distance ÷ Time
Remember to convert minutes to hours first!

Example: 32°F → (32-32) × 5/9 = 0 × 5/9 = 0°C (freezing!)

👗 Planning a photoshoot. Weather says 45°F.
What is that in °C? (round to nearest tenth)

💡 First subtract 32 from 45. Then multiply by 5/9.

🚚 A delivery van drives 40 km in 2 hours and 30 minutes.
What is the average rate in km/hr?

💡 Convert 2h 30m to hours first: 2.5h. Then divide 40 by 2.5.

📐 Section 10: Area & Perimeter

Triangle: A = ½ × base × height
Rectangle area: A = length × width
Rectangle perimeter: P = 2l + 2w

Example: Rectangle area = 20, length = 5. Find width.
20 = 5 × w → w = 20 ÷ 5 = 4

🎨 A triangular canvas has area = 25.2 in² and base = 8 in.
Find the height.

💡 25.2 = (1/2)(8)(h) = 4h. So h = 25.2 ÷ 4

👗 A rectangular fabric piece has area = 57 ft² and length = 12 ft.
Find the width.

💡 57 = 12 × w. So w = 57 ÷ 12

Find the width.
