🔥 HEAT

Heat is controlled by individual room thermostats. These thermostats control the heat only. Please be sure to have them shut off is running the A/C.

❄️ A/C

There are multiple window A/C units. It is suggested you run all units on hot days or it will be difficult to keep the downstairs at a comfortable temperature in the late afternoon.

🔥 FIREPLACE

Remote-operated. Press and hold the two buttons connected by the line with the flame on it (bottom left and top right) at the same time. The unit will beep and click — it takes about 20 seconds for it to go through the lighting process. Once lit, please immediately lower the flame using the down-flame button or the house will get very hot.

PLEASE DO NOT TOUCH LOG SET FOR ANY REASON. Please do not use the fireplace while running the A/C.
