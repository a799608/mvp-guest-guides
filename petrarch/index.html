🌡️ Climate Control

🔥 Heat

Wall thermostats are for heat only. Turn them OFF before running A/C.

❄️ Air Conditioning

Three units — one in each queen bedroom and one in the living room.

Run all three units. Running only 1 or 2 will not cool the home adequately.

🔥 Fireplace

Remote-operated. To start, press and hold the two buttons connected by the line with the flame on it (bottom left and top right) at the same time. The unit will beep and click — it takes a few seconds to light. Once lit, immediately lower the flame using the down-flame button or the house will get very hot.

DO NOT TOUCH anything other than the remote. DO NOT TOUCH the log set. Non-operational May to August. Do not use the fireplace while running the A/C. Non-venting gas — not intended as a primary heat source.

🚪 Front Door & Pet Gates

🔑 Front Door Lock

The front door has a special mechanism. To lock or unlock, you must turn the lock AND the handle at the same time. Clockwise to lock, counter-clockwise to unlock. From outside, turn the key and handle at the same time. There is a key on the kitchen counter if you prefer to use the front door during your stay.

🐶 Pet Gates

Retractable gates are installed at the front door (to the right, attaches to the top post like a window shade) and at the bottom of the interior stairs. Useful for containing pets while still allowing you to enter and exit.

🍳 Kitchen

🌫️ Hood Vent

Please run the hood vent on high any time you’re cooking or using the stove. It vents outside and helps reduce strong food odors like bacon and curry.

🌀 Garbage Disposal

Works via a push-button toggle switch on the front of the sink cabinet. Please don’t dispose of large quantities of food.
