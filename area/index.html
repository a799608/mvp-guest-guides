Towamensing Trails — Albrightsville, PA

Welcome to the Poconos! This guide covers the community amenities (pool, beach, clubhouse, courts) and the best local spots to explore during your stay. Tap any “View on Map” link to open directions on your phone.

🏖️ Community Amenities

🏢 Community Office & Trash Center

The office handles community questions. All trash and recycling goes to the collection center — it cannot be left outside because of bears.

🌊 Beach

Community beach on the lake. A great spot to relax and let the kids play. Wristbands required for anyone over age 5.

View on Map

🏊 Pool

Community pool with a lifeguarded area. Wristbands required for anyone over age 5. Extra day-use bands are $10 at the pool gate.

View on Map

🍽️ Clubhouse & Restaurant

The community clubhouse has a casual restaurant for grabbing a bite without leaving the community. Please note: alcohol cannot be purchased on-site.

View on Map

⛵ Marina & Fishing Dock

Boat access and fishing dock on the lake. Non-powered boat rentals may be available seasonally — check with the office.

Marina Boat Rentals

🎾 Sports & Courts

Tennis, pickleball, basketball, sand volleyball, and horseshoes are all within the community. Courts are first-come, first-served.

Tennis / Pickleball Basketball Sand Volleyball Horseshoes
There is also a baseball/softball field, but it is currently not in great shape.

🗺️ Local Favorites

🛒 Dollar General

The closest spot for groceries, essentials, beer, and charcoal for the grill. A few restaurants and a gas station are nearby too.

View on Map

🌳 Weiser State Forest

Great hiking with few people. Peaceful and much less trafficked than the state parks — a favorite for a quiet morning walk.

View on Map

🥾 Hickory Run State Park

Popular state park with well-maintained hiking trails and the famous Boulder Field. Expect more visitors, but the scenery is worth it.

View on Map

🛶 Pocono Whitewater

Whitewater rafting on the Lehigh River. A great half-day adventure for families and groups.

View on Map

🎯 Skirmish Paintball

One of the largest paintball parks in the country. Fun for bigger groups and older kids.

View on Map

🚄 Town of Jim Thorpe

Historic mountain town packed with restaurants, shops, train rides, and outdoor adventures. If you only visit one place outside the community, make it this one.

View on Map

🏖️ Lake Harmony

A scenic lake community with restaurants and shops. Good for a relaxed day trip.

View on Map
