🚽 SEPTIC SYSTEM

Septic-safe toilet paper only. No wipes, hygiene products, or feminine products. These will cause serious damage to the septic system.

🔑 FRONT DOOR & PET GATES

Front Door: Front door has a passcode lock that auto-locks. Do not forget your code when leaving. The code is 0119.

Pet Gates: There are two gates on the deck to contain pets to deck, or to allow access to fenced yard.

🚫 THE NOS
  • No smoking inside home (including vaping and marijuana). Outside is fine but please away from open windows.
  • No fireworks ($1000 fine)
  • No noise after 10 PM (quiet hours 10 PM – 8 AM)
🗑️ TRASH COLLECTION
  • Community does not allow curbside pickup due to bears
  • All trash must be taken to the community collection center

Display mirror tag/parking pass at the collection center to access

PLEASE DO NOT TOUCH LOG SET FOR ANY REASON. Please do not use the fireplace while running the A/C.
